How to Play Spaceman

Your objective, if you accept, is to keep the Spaceman from leaving the planet! He has agreed to stay under one condition, you guess the word he is thinking. It's almost like you're Amy Adams in the movie, "Arrival" but at the same time, not really.

To make it a little bit easier on yourself, he is allowing you to guess one letter at a time, but be careful! You can only make six mistakes before he takes off, leaving you as confused as I was at the end of "Arrival".

There will be no, "Houston, we have a problem" here, but just incase there is, you can restart whenever you'd like!

Now go be like the crazy guy in Independence day and make us all proud. Best of luck!
