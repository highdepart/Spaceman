How to Play Spaceman

Your objective, if you accept, is to keep our Astronaut on earth! The goal of the game is to guess the hidden word, by guessing letters. If too many letters do not appear in the word, our Astronaut will take off in his/her ship and you will lose.

You can have as many guesses as you would like but once you guess six letters incorrectly, our Astronaut leaves orbit!
